Welcome to HYBRON

Our product portfolio is comprised of Injection Mould, Mould Insert, Core & Cavity, Progressive tool, Press tool inserts-Die & Punch, Jigs & Fixture and Precision Machined Components. We offer top quality products with unparalleled technical assistance and value addition with outstanding customer satisfaction.

About HYBRON

HYBRON was started in 2024, by a group of three people Mr. Binosh Kizhakkakara Koshy, Mr Nizamudeen CV and Mr.Rafnas started in Banglore in view to develop an import substitute engineering items as being required to various Multi-National Companies.
